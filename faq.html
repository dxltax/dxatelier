Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of projects do you usually work on?

I mostly work on modern, responsive websites using React, TailwindCSS, and TypeScript. I specialize in clean UI/UX and frontend functionality, from landing pages to web apps.

Do you offer design services as well?

While I focus on frontend development, I can collaborate with designers or use provided Figma/Adobe files. If needed, I can also help with basic layout design using Tailwind.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope, but for standard websites, 3–7 days is common. Larger or custom apps may take 1–4 weeks.

What are your rates?

I offer flexible pricing depending on project needs. You can check my Booking page for full packages and benefits.

Do you provide after-project support?

Yes. I offer 7–14 days of free maintenance after project delivery and longer-term support if requested.

Can I request revisions after the project is delivered?

Of course. Reasonable revisions are welcome and included in most packages.

What tools and languages do you use?

I mainly use React, TailwindCSS, JavaScript/TypeScript, and Git. I'm also familiar with Next.js, basic Firebase, and other frontend tools.

How do I get started?

Just reach out via WhatsApp or email. We’ll discuss your project, goals, and timelines.
